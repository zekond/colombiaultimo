RECIPES

We are a family owned and operated restaurant. Our family moved to LongWood, Florida 15 years ago from Bogota Colombia . We are so proud of our gastronomy that we opened our restaurant 12 years ago, with a little help from my grandmother's recipes. Our grandmother is so proud that her recipes have not been changed. Even though she has never been in this country, in a way she is with us every day.I would like to thank all of our patrons who have supported me for the last 12 years and all to come. Just come in and have a real Colombian Entrée at Los Colombia La Bella! Where we celebrate our tradition every day!
